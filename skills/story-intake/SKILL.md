# Story Intake

## Purpose

Find or accept target stories, present a triage view, assess minimum compliance, and stop only after producing a structured handoff for refinement.

## Entry Modes

- `discovery`
- `explicit`

## Required Outputs

- story triage list
- open questions
- compliance diagnosis
- compliance task draft when needed
- refinement handoff

## Completion Gate

- selected story
- triage output shown
- compliance evaluated
- handoff generated

## ADO Write Rules

- draft first
- require confirmation before apply
- English only
- no AI disclosure
